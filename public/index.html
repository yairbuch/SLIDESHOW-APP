ABOUT SLIDESHOW

Welcome to SLIDESHOW!
Here you can publish your pictures to our growing community and get the best price to your gallery.
Our Web Application is friendly for users and all you need is just few steps to connect our server and start working.

First step ,you have to sign up and fill the added form in the 'signup page'. The necessary fields have asterisk and you must fill them. For example, here you must add your name but not your address details: Yet, if you choose to fill the optinal fields too, you have to fill them as well in accordance the remarks that will show beneath the input field. After you will fill all the required fields as well, the 'Submit' button become accesible and you can click it.

Second step , after you signed up please move to LOGIN PAGE and log in with the email and password you regiested with. In addition, you can log in by using one of our users email's: regular@gmail.com , business@gmail.com, admin@gmail.com. The password for all this users is : Aa1234!.
You can see your regiested details in the console and copy your email and password.



Now, when you are log in, the fitcher 'Add Pic' will apear up in the navbar, and you can get in and insert your picture's details and present it in the site. The new picture will join to the end of the slideshow:
